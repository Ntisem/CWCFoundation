About Us

Who we are

Cilla World Charity (CWC) Foundation CWC Foundation is a Ghanaian based non-profit organization, which was founded in March 2020 by Priscilla Andoh, a native of Bawku in the Upper East Region of Ghana, West Africa. We focus on providing care and support for less privileged children and women in our communities. and as the Nation as whole.

Charity has always been a part of her and being able to give back to society has been her greatest joy. She met Henry, who believe in her dreams and also had the passion for giving, that was when Cilla World Charities Foundation came to a reality.

  • Our Mission

    The aim of this foundation is to care, support and provide for Children,women and the needy in our communities.

  • Our Vision

    Projects we undertake benefit the less privileged children amd women and the community as a whole.

  • Our Goals
    • To ensure survival, protection, and development of all children. To make them take active part in the lives of their families, schools, and communities.
