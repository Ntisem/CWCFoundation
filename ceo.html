Message from CEO

# THE FOUNDER

My Name is Priscilla Andoh, I am a daughter and a sister to my family. Am a Midwifery Student, a make artist, model and an Entrepreneur. At a young age I learned and understood the meaning of giving and that has been part of me. It has been a dream for me to give back to society and to help the less privillage.

I am an ordianry lady who believe that ordinary people can do extraordinary things if they believe in themselves and take a step towards it. I am not different from any other young lady out there but what set me apart is my passion to help children and women and to put smiles on their faces and to make this world a better place. It fills my heart with joy and happiness to start on this journey with my Cilla World Charities Foundation (CWC Foundation). I know with hard work, determination and dedication my dream of giving will come through and with my help I will put smiles on the faces pf people amd make this world a better place.
